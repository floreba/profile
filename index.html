  • Toilet Finder

    Spotaloo has been a group project in the LeWagon course I took. You can checkout the Github Repo. It lets you find, rate & add toilets of your choice in your area. George Costanza would've loved that website.

    We designed it with "mobile first" in mind, on a full screen it might look silly in certain areas. You are more than invited to upload some toilets in your area to enrich everybody's experience who is in need of a toilet. Although it's been a "fun" project I definitely learned a lot during the project. Shoutout to my all-italian speaking teammates Ricardo, Andrea & Sebastian with whom I had the pleasure of working with.
